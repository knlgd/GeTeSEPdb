The online service facilitates the prediction of expression trends for genes of transcriptomic data. This service currently exclusively accommodates input consisting of expression profile data and temporal sequence information.

Note:

  • We recommend uploading files for analysis. In the sample information table, the first column should be named "gene_data.txt," and the other two columns can be modeled after the example data.The data failing to meet the input criteria cannot be analyzed or displayed.
  • An adjusted R^2 value less than 0.7 after fitting indicates analysis failure.
